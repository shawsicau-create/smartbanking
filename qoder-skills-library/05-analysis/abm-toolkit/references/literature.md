## 1. ABM方法论（必读）

### 1.1 综述与教材

3. **Railsback, S.F. & Grimm, V. (2019)** - Agent-Based and Individual-Based Modeling: A Practical Introduction. Princeton University Press.
   - ABM方法论标准教材

   - NetLogo ABM入门教材

5. **Epstein, J.M. & Axtell, R. (1996)** - Growing Artificial Societies: Social Science from the Bottom Up. MIT Press.
   - ABM社会科学应用先驱

6. **Bonabeau, E. (2002)** - Agent-based modeling: Methods and techniques for simulating human systems. *PNAS*, 99(suppl 3), 7280-7287.
   - ABM方法论综述
